The Arachnology Lab, headed by Lorenzo Prendini, Associate Curator (Arachnids and Myriapods), includes permanent specialists, various laboratory assistants, postdoctoral fellows, and Ph.D. students. Every year, the Group accommodates several visiting scientists, undergraduate students, high school students, and volunteers.
